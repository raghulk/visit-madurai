Places to shop in Madurai

Vishaal De Mal

Vishaal De Mal has a humongous area under its purview that is spread over 230,000 square feet. Having multiple theme parks, cinemas, shops and restaurants, this mall is a complete delight to visit. It is this mall which has initiated the large format mall culture in southern Tamil Nadu. The mall has a 10,000 square feet play area with games like dashing cars and bowling. It has a 12,000 square feet food court, which includes Caf√© Coffee Day and Pizza Corner. The mall houses department stores, apparel, and jewelry shops.

Masi Street

Talking of shopping hubs of the city, there always is a distinct place where the youth of the city find solace and that is exactly what the Masi Street is for Madurai. Dealing with fashion accessories this market is hoarded by the young people in the city.

Puthu Mandapam

Witness the tailors right there on the street sewing their bright-hued collection of products. Nestled on the eastern side of the Meenakshi Temple, this market basically deals in metal, bronze and aluminum vessels, but the hoarding presence of the tailors cannot be ignored. This is among the markets that remain crowded through the week. The market is full of shops dealing with handicrafts, textiles and metallurgical articles. The skilled tailors at the textile center, reproduce garments from cotton and printed fabrics within a couple of hours.
