Madurai is one of the ancient cities of South India with a glorious history. It is famous for its world acclaimed Meenakshi Sundareswarar Temple. It is known for its rich historical landmarks such as Thirumalai Nayakar Palace. The city is known as the "The city that never sleeps" as shops here remain open overnight on all days. It is located on the banks of River Vaigai and has been a major settlement for two millennia.
